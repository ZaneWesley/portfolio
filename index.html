About.

Hey There! I'm Zane, a web dev. I love Jesus, people, web development, and Apple. I specialize in front-end UX and UI design. My mission is to have a positive impact in the life of everyone I encounter through everyday interactions.

In 2019, I created my first website. Inspired by all the creative portfolios out there, I wanted to create my own. From there, my journey began. To this day, I continue run a personal website for fun, which has following my learning journey.

Currently I attend Union University (class of 2027) to attain a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in web development and a minor in business marketing.

My main focus these days is simply to pass my classes. But recently, I have also been given various opportunities to freelance web development. Additionally, in my spare time, I work at Chick-fil-a and volunteer at my local church, Love and Truth Church.

Projects.

Love and Truth Church needed a survey built to help guide its members towards their biblical spiritual gifts during the Next Steps process. This survey includes a DISC personality profile with a coresponding description. Results also include examples of people woth this same personality and a list of possible ways to better oneself.

The survey also provides a list of the top 5 possible spiritual gifts based on the answers to the questions, giving a description and supporting scripture.

The results page ends with a functional save button that converts the page to a downloadable PDF.
